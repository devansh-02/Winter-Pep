About Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players on a field at the center of which is a 22-yard pitch. It is one of the most popular sports in the world, known for its rich traditions and exciting matches.

Basic Rules of Cricket

  • The game is played between two teams of eleven players each.
  • Each team takes turns batting and bowling.
  • The goal of the batting team is to score runs, while the bowling team tries to dismiss the batsmen.
  • The match can be played in different formats, such as Test, ODI, or T20.
